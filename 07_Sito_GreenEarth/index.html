ABOUT

Who we are

GreenEarth is a start-up committed to the reforestation and cleanup of the seas and oceans, with the goal of protecting and restoring natural ecosystems through sustainable and innovative initiatives.

Green Earth is composed of experts in ecology, marine biology, environmental management and volunteers, all united to safeguard the Earth.

OUR MISSION

EcoFuture's mission is to reforest degraded lands, clean up oceans and educate communities about the importance of environmental sustainability. Through targeted projects and strategic partnerships, the company aims to combat climate change and protect the planet's biodiversity.

OUR PROJECTS

Reforest the Amazon, Brasil

We plant native trees in the Amazon Rainforest to combat deforestation, restore biodiversity and absorb CO2, working with local communities.

Beach Cleanup in Bacolod, Philippines

We organize cleanup days on Bacolod Beach to remove plastic and marine debris, raising awareness within the local community about the importance of keeping coastal ecosystems clean..

Reforestation in Tanzania

We initiate reforestation projects in Tanzania, planting trees in degraded lands to improve soil quality, protect biodiversity, and support local economies.
